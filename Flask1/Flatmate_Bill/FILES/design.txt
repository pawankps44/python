Title: Flatmates_bill
Description:The app gets inputs as the names of the flatmates and how many days they stayed in the flat,
the total bill for the particular period and returns how much they have to pay.It should generate a pdf file
where it has flatmates names and how much they have to pay,total bill and the period of the bill.
objects:
        flatmates:
                names
                no of days stayed

        bill:
            total bill
            period
            pays(flatmates)

        pdf:
            name
            generate(flatmate1,flatmate2,bill)
